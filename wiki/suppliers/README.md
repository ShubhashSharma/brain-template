# Suppliers

One page per factory or trader. Linked from every SKU they make.

---

## Why this folder is gold
Most operators carry supplier knowledge in their head, in WhatsApp threads, and in a few scattered spreadsheets. The first time a key person leaves, that knowledge walks out the door.

A supplier page captures: terms, MOQs, lead times, defect history, sample log, contacts. Plain text. Searchable. Cloneable when you onboard a backup factory.

---

## Naming
`country-name-or-business.md`. Examples:
- `shenzhen-soft-goods-co.md`
- `india-jaipur-textiles.md`
- `vietnam-binh-duong-leather.md`

---

## Status field meanings

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `active` | Currently producing for you |
| `shadow` | Backup. Sampled, qualified, not yet placing orders |
| `dormant` | Used in the past, available again if needed |
| `dropped` | Do not use. Reason in the page body |

## What to capture every time you place an order
Append to the supplier page. Don't make a new page per order.
- PO number, units, price tier, ship date, defect rate after receipt.
