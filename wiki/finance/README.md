# Finance

Monthly P&L closes, cash plan, restock budget. The numbers that drive every other decision in the vault.

---

## What lives here
- `monthly-close-YYYY-MM.md` - one per month, use `_templates/monthly-close.md`
- `cash-plan-YYYY-Q{1-4}.md` - quarterly cash forecast
- `restock-plan-YYYY-MM.md` - monthly restock budget allocation

---

## The one rule
Numbers in this folder must reconcile with your bank statements and your channel payouts. If they don't, the monthly close is fiction and `/wbr` will lie to you.

---

## When to update
- Monthly close: by the 5th of each month for the prior month.
- Cash plan: at the start of each quarter, then adjust if a major variance hits.
- Restock plan: at the start of each month, refreshed weekly.
