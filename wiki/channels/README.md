# Channels

One page per sales channel. Same SKU on Amazon US and TikTok Shop US has different fees, different ad mechanics, different CM3.

---

## Why a separate folder
Operators routinely run a SKU at 35% CM3 on Amazon and 12% CM3 on TikTok and treat it as one number. It's not. Channel pages force you to see the split.

---

## Pre-stubbed channels
Four common channels are pre-stubbed for you:
- `amazon-us.md`
- `amazon-uk.md`
- `tiktok-shop-us.md`
- `shopify.md`

Fill in your account IDs and live SKUs. Add more (Walmart, eBay, etsy) using `_templates/channel.md`.

---

## What to revisit monthly
- Refresh the 30-day numbers block.
- Check Brand Registry / Brand Store / A+ status (Amazon channels).
- Check creator commission tier and top creators (TikTok Shop).
- Check theme + app stack drift (Shopify).
