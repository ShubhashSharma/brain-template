## UK-specific things to remember
- VAT: 20% on most goods. Make sure you are VAT-registered if you breach the £85K threshold.
- Importer of Record: required post-Brexit. Either UK Ltd entity or a service like Avask/Avalara.
- UKCA / CE marking: depending on category, both may apply during transition.
- Pricing: list in GBP, not USD-converted. Re-localise listing copy (en-GB spelling, UK colloquialisms).
- Reviews: lower review counts in general. Vine is available but smaller pool.
