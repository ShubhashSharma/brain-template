## DTC-specific things to remember
- **CAC vs CM3:** if blended CAC > 0.5 of CM3 per order, the unit economics are stressed.
- **First-purchase vs repeat:** 60d / 90d / 365d LTV by SKU drives whether paid acquisition is sustainable.
- **Pixel + CAPI:** both required for trustworthy attribution. iOS 14.5+ broke client-side tracking.
- **Email/SMS list value:** typical $1-3 per subscriber per year, depending on category.
