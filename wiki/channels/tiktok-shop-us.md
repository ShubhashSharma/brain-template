## TikTok-specific things to remember
- **Affiliate creator commission:** typical 10-30%. Set per SKU. Front-of-mind because it stacks on top of channel fees.
- **GMV vs revenue:** TikTok reports GMV (gross). Subtract returns, refunds, cancellations to get revenue.
- **Returns rate:** consistently higher than Amazon. Plan 8-15% baseline.
- **No per-order ad attribution.** TikTok ads attribute at campaign x day level. CM3 per-SKU requires Pixel/CAPI on storefront, not in TikTok admin. (See [[wiki/concepts/]] entry for tiktok-shop-attribution if added.)
- **Top creators:** identify and protect them. 80% of GMV often comes from 5-10 creators per SKU.
- **Live shopping:** schedule, not optional, for SKUs above $50 ASP.
