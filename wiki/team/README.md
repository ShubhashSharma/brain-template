# Team

Anyone you work with. Internal team, China sourcing, US VAs, UK ops, agencies, freelancers.

---

## Naming
`firstname-lastname.md`, or `agency-name.md`.

---

## Why centralise here
Two reasons:

1. **Onboarding the next hire.** Your COO / CFO / second-in-command will need to learn who does what. The team folder is the answer.
2. **Knowing where decisions die.** Decision pages link to owners. If a person has 14 open decisions and is on holiday, you can see it.

---

## Status field
Use `active`, `paused`, or `former`. Don't delete former contractors, you'll need their context if they come back or if there's a dispute.
