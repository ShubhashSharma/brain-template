# Acme Baby Shampoo - move from $19.99 to $22.99

Lift list price 15% on the parent ASIN to test elasticity. CM3 currently 24%, target 30%+.

---

## Context
Through Q4 2025 and Jan 2026, conversion rate held above 14% even as competitors moved into the $22-25 band. Search-rank position 6-8 on the head term, BSR top 30 in subcategory. Reviews 4.4 with 3,000+ count: brand equity is doing work.

CM3 at $19.99 is 24%. Three SKUs in the line are dragging total CM3 below 28% target. A successful price lift here pulls the line up and funds the lotion launch.

---

## Options considered

| Option | Pros | Cons | Estimated impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| A. Hold at $19.99 | Zero risk, conversion stays | CM3 stays at 24%, line target missed | $0 |
| B. Move to $21.99 | Small lift, low risk | Likely under-shoots elasticity ceiling | +$2,400/mo CM3 |
| C. (chosen) Move to $22.99 | Captures full elasticity room, lifts CM3 to ~30% | Some conversion risk if competitors react | +$5,800/mo CM3 |
| D. Move to $24.99 | Maximises CM3 if elasticity holds | Crosses competitor pricing threshold | Risky, untested |

---

## Decision
Move list price to $22.99 effective 2026-02-05, hold for 30 days, measure conversion + units + CM3, decide on permanence at follow-up.

---

## Reversibility
- One-way door: **no**, price can be reverted in seconds.
- If conversion drops more than 15% over 30 days at the new price, revert to $19.99.
- Signal we are wrong: units/day drops below 35 (was 40-45 at $19.99) for two consecutive weeks.
