# BFCM (Black Friday / Cyber Monday)

The five-day window from US Thanksgiving Thursday through Cyber Monday. The biggest single-period of revenue for most multi-channel ecom operators.

---

## When to run
- Trigger: Q3 starts (1 July). BFCM planning is a 20-week build.
- Critical: stock-and-asset deadlines all sit between T-12 and T-6 weeks.

---

## Pre-event checklist

### T-20 weeks (early July)
- [ ] Set BFCM revenue target by channel. Build top-down from Q4 plan.
- [ ] Pick hero SKUs per channel. Amazon, TikTok Shop, Shopify each need their own.
- [ ] Lock supplier POs for BFCM cover. Lead times stretch in Q3 due to factory backlog.

### T-12 weeks (early September)
- [ ] All BFCM-bound inventory ON A BOAT or AT A 3PL by end of week.
- [ ] FBA inbound limits checked. Restock plan confirmed.
- [ ] Creator / affiliate briefs sent for TikTok Shop and Shopify (long lead time on content).

### T-8 weeks (early October)
- [ ] Email + SMS flows built and tested in Klaviyo / Postscript.
- [ ] Brand Store and Amazon Posts refreshed.
- [ ] TikTok Shop top SKU pages refreshed (variants, pricing, A+ equivalent).
- [ ] PPC budget envelope locked. Plan +200% to +300% daily during event.

### T-4 weeks (late October)
- [ ] All ad creative locked (no Day-of edits).
- [ ] Discount / promo structure approved by Operator + Finance.
- [ ] Sales tax exposure check complete (BFCM volume sometimes triggers nexus thresholds).
- [ ] Customer service team briefed on expected ticket volume.

### T-2 weeks
- [ ] Final inventory check.
- [ ] Daily ops cadence agreed (who watches what, when).
- [ ] Out-of-office: nobody who matters is on holiday across the 5 days.

### Day-of (each of the 5 days)
- [ ] 06:00 local: yesterday's numbers, OOS check, refund spike check.
- [ ] Hourly through 22:00: hero SKU stock + buy box + ad spend pacing.
- [ ] End of day: snapshot, plan tomorrow's adjustments.

---

## During the event - what to watch
- **Multi-channel cannibalisation.** Same SKU at $19.99 on Amazon and $17.99 on Shopify will see Amazon shoppers price-comparing.
- **Cyber Monday tail.** Tuesday-Wednesday post-CM is often quiet but week 49-50 picks up again. Don't kill ad budgets too soon.
- **Refund / return spikes after Dec 25.** Hold cash buffer for refunds through 15 January.

## What NOT to do
- Don't launch new SKUs the week before BFCM.
- Don't change packaging mid-event.
- Don't trust last-year's playbook without updating it (Amazon fee changes, TikTok algorithm changes, etc).

---

## Post-event retro

Capture by 5 December:
- Revenue by channel vs target
- CM3 by channel for the 5-day window
- Top 5 SKUs by absolute CM3 contribution
- Bottom 5 SKUs - did discount destroy CM3?
- Inventory burn vs plan
- Customer service ticket volume + top issues
- What we'd do differently next year

Update this playbook in place with the lessons. The next operator who reads it (or you, in 11 months) needs them.
