# Prime Day

Annual two-day Amazon event, typically mid-July (US). Often a second one in October ("Prime Big Deal Days"). Treat both with this playbook.

---

## When to run
- Trigger: Amazon announces dates (usually 4-6 weeks out)
- Lead time required: 8-10 weeks if Lightning Deals submitted, 4-6 weeks if not

---

## Pre-event checklist

### T-10 weeks
- [ ] Pick 3-5 hero SKUs. Criteria: BSR top 3 in subcategory, CM3 above 25%, 1,000+ reviews, healthy stock.
- [ ] Check FBA inbound capacity for each hero SKU. Need 4 weeks of Prime Day cover IN FBA by T-3 weeks.
- [ ] Submit Prime Exclusive Discounts (PED) and Lightning Deals if eligible.

### T-8 weeks
- [ ] Place restock POs sized for Prime Day + 8 weeks post-event tail.
- [ ] Confirm supplier can ship by T-5 weeks at the latest.
- [ ] Brief PPC agency / in-house ads on Prime Day budget envelope (typically 2-3x daily spend during event).

### T-4 weeks
- [ ] Refresh hero SKU listings: hero image, A+ content, bullet 1.
- [ ] Check Brand Story is current.
- [ ] Verify all hero SKUs have at least 100 reviews in the last 90 days.

### T-2 weeks
- [ ] Build deal pages on Brand Store.
- [ ] Pre-write social / email creator briefs.
- [ ] Set Sponsored Brands video creative for hero SKUs.

### T-1 week
- [ ] Final inventory check, FBA receivable confirmed.
- [ ] PPC campaigns moved to Prime-Day budget envelope (auto-trigger on Day 1 morning).
- [ ] Brief the team on day-of ops Slack channel.

### Day-of
- [ ] 06:00 local: pull yesterday's numbers, check no listing surprises.
- [ ] Hourly: check stock levels on hero SKUs. If any drops to <2 days at current velocity, kill the deal early to avoid OOS suppression.
- [ ] Hourly: check buy box ownership.
- [ ] End-of-day: snapshot full numbers, write tomorrow's adjustments.

---

## During the event - what to watch
- **Buy box loss.** Competitors with stock can win on price even with worse reviews.
- **Stock outs.** Running out mid-day triggers OOS penalty that lasts weeks.
- **Ad spend velocity.** Daily spend can hit your envelope by 11am if you didn't cap.
- **Refund spikes.** First-day refunds often signal a listing claim issue, not a product issue.

## What NOT to do
- Don't launch a new SKU during Prime Day. The data is unrepresentative.
- Don't change listing copy mid-event. Wait until after.
- Don't reduce ad spend if early ROAS looks bad. Day 1 amortises into Day 2 organic lift.
