# Playbooks

Repeatable event runbooks. Pre-event checklist, what to watch during, post-event retro.

---

## Shipped playbooks
- `prime-day.md`
- `bfcm.md` (Black Friday + Cyber Monday)
- `restock-cycle.md` (the weekly Mon ritual)
- `new-launch.md` (new SKU launch ladder)

Add your own (Q5, review-crisis, listing-suppression-recovery, etc) using `_templates/playbook.md`.

---

## How to actually use a playbook
1. Read it the week before the event.
2. Copy the checklist into your daily note.
3. After the event, run the retro section. Update the playbook in place with what you learned.

The playbook gets sharper every cycle. That compounding is the point.
